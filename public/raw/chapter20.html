20.1 How to Use This Chapter 使い方

Over 500 phrases, grouped by situation. This is the chapter to keep open on your phone in the hospital corridor. Everything is in polite です・ます form unless marked, so you can say any of it to any member of staff.

The five phrases that matter most: if you learn nothing else, learn these.

20.25 Final Word 最後に

You are going to be fine

You will not understand everything that is said to you in that hospital. You do not need to. The nurses and midwives of Japan are extraordinarily kind, and they have guided thousands of frightened, exhausted fathers who spoke less Japanese than you do. Say 「すみません」, say 「お願いします」, hold your wife's hand, and let them lead.

そして、赤ちゃんが生まれたら — 「本当にありがとうございました。」 Soshite, akachan ga umaretara — hontō ni arigatō gozaimashita. And when the baby is born — thank you, from the bottom of my heart.
